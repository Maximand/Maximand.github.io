WHY THIS FILE (read the README): the theme's full-bleed cover banner is NOT rendered here — it is an emitted by _layouts/page.html whenever a post's front matter sets `article_header.type: cover`. So replacing this include can never remove the banner. The correct, theme-native way to get the target full-bleed *typographic* hero (above the content + TOC grid, no photo) is `article_header.type: overlay`.
